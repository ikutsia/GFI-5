About GFI

The Georgian Film Institute, a non-profit, serves as a unifying body for professionals across various fields within the film industry. It formulates a comprehensive film policy shaped by individuals collaborating within the Board of Filmmakers and the GFI Member base.

The film policy is rooted in the fundamental values of the Institute. Its primary objective revolves around the advancement, bolstering, and advocacy of contemporary Georgian film.
